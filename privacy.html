Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Silky Road Marketplace ("Silky Road", "we", "us") collects, uses and protects your personal information when you use our website and services.

2. Information we collect

  • Account information: Email address, password and basic profile details when you sign up.
  • Authentication data: If you log in with Google, we receive basic profile and email details from Google via Supabase Auth.
  • Transaction data: When you buy products, our payment partners (such as Stripe or PayPal) process your payment details. We do not store full card numbers.
  • Usage data: IP address, browser type, device information and pages visited to help us improve the marketplace.

3. How we use your information

  • To create and manage your account.
  • To deliver purchased digital products and manage your library.
  • To process payments and payouts through third-party providers.
  • To prevent fraud, abuse and security issues.
  • To communicate updates, support messages and important notices.

4. Cookies and analytics

We may use cookies and similar technologies to keep you signed in, remember preferences and measure site performance. You can usually control cookies via your browser settings.

5. Data storage

Silky Road uses cloud services such as Supabase for authentication, database and file storage. Your data may be processed and stored in data centers outside your country, subject to appropriate protections.

6. Sharing your information

We do not sell your personal data. We may share limited information with:

  • Payment processors (for example, Stripe or PayPal) to handle payments and payouts.
  • Email providers (for example, Resend or similar) to send account and support emails.
  • Service providers that help maintain and secure the platform.
  • Authorities, if required by law or to protect our rights.

7. Your rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, update or delete your personal data, or to object to certain processing. You can contact us via the Help > Support page to make such requests.

8. Data retention

We keep your information for as long as your account is active or as needed for legal, accounting and security purposes. When no longer required, personal data may be anonymized or deleted.

9. Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data. However, no online service can guarantee absolute security.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make important changes, we aim to notify users via email or on-site notices.

11. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us via the Help > Support page.
